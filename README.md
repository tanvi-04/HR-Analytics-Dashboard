Employee attrition is a critical challenge for organizations. This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee data to uncover patterns related to attrition and retention.

The dashboard answers questions such as:

* Which departments experience the highest attrition?
* How does attrition vary by age group, gender, and education level?
* What is the relationship between job satisfaction, work-life balance, and attrition?
* How does job level and performance impact employee turnover?
